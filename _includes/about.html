About

Our mission is to help incoming and current Indians in Tokyo Institute of Technology and introduce others to Indian culture.

ISAT is a not-for-profit students' association in Tokyo Institute of Technology, Japan. ISAT works as the platform for connecting and supporting current and incoming students at the University, in particular students of Indian origin.

As a representative of India, the group shall also aim at promoting and introducing Indian culture among other students through various activities like get-togethers, day trips, workshops, and cultural events. The association shall commit to the objectives of the Tokyo Tech International Students' Association (TISA).
